Tools & Apps

Anki

Anki is a flashcard app that lets you create flashcards about anything you want to remember. Anki will then ask you to review them at the most optimal time (this is called an SRS, spaced repetition system). It is a brilliant way make sure that you don't forget anything that you learn.

Here are some guides on the best ways to use Anki:
  • How to make effective flashcards

Subs2SRS

Subs2SRS is a utility that that takes a video and subtitles file, and chunks the video up based on the subtitle timings, creating Anki SRS cards with an audio clip, screenshot, and the associated line of dialog. If you're into Anki, it's a fantastic way to break into watching Japanese shows or practicing your listening.
